This project was made with Constituting America.

Constituting America is a non-profit organization that helps Americans—especially students—learn about the U.S. Constitution and why it matters today. It was founded by actress and political commentator Janine Turner along with co-chair Cathy Gillespie. The group offers educational programs, podcasts, contests, and free resources to help people understand how the Constitution protects our freedoms and shapes our government.

Each year, they host a scholarship contest where students can submit essays, videos, songs, and artwork about the Constitution for a chance to win prizes and national recognition. Their goal is to inspire young Americans to be active, informed citizens.
